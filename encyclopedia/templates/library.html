The Mesozoic Era

The Mesozoic Era, also known as the "Age of Reptiles," lasted from about 252 to 66 million years ago. It is divided into three periods: the Triassic, Jurassic, and Cretaceous.

Did You Know?

  • Dinosaurs ruled the Earth for over 165 million years!

  • Birds are the living descendants of theropod dinosaurs.
  • The name "dinosaur" means "terrible lizard" in Greek.
